Elite Real Estate Group is a private residential developer. We buy the land, design the architecture, build the community and stand behind every home we hand over.

Our Story

From one building to a portfolio of communities

Elite began in 2009 with a single apartment building in New Cairo and a simple conviction: homes should be designed with the same rigor as landmark architecture, and delivered with the discipline of an engineering firm.

Today we develop entire communities — masterplans, landscapes, retail and residences — with our own design studio and construction management team. Because we develop and sell only our own projects, our name is on every promise we make.

Our Mission

To design and deliver residential communities that improve everyday life — combining architectural quality, honest pricing and on-time handover for every family and investor who trusts us.

Our Vision

To be the developer families recommend to each other — recognized across the region for precision, transparency and communities that grow more valuable with time.
