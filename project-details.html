Project Overview

A community designed around daily life

Elite Residence is our flagship development in the heart of the Fifth Settlement — a gated community where architecture, landscape and services work together.

Across 32 acres, eight-storey buildings frame a sequence of landscaped courtyards, keeping 70% of the masterplan open and green. Apartments are oriented for daylight and privacy, finished to a high standard, and served by a retail promenade, clubhouse and full sports facilities. Every detail — from lobby materials to smart home readiness — is specified by our in-house design team and delivered by our own construction arm.

Location

Minutes from everything that matters

Directly off 90th Street South with immediate access to the Ring Road — 5 minutes to AUC, 10 minutes to Cairo Festival City, 25 minutes to Cairo International Airport.
